Project Idea

WellAware is an AI-powered app that analyzes simple user data to detect early signs of common health issues. It helps young people take preventive steps before small problems become serious.

Problem Being Solved

Many young people miss early warning signs of health problems due to infrequent checkups. WellAware provides personalized awareness and guides users toward preventive action.
